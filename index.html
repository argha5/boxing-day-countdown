Frequently Asked Questions

When is Boxing Day 2026?

Boxing Day 2026 falls on Saturday, December 26, 2026. This is the day after Christmas and marks the beginning of major post-holiday sales.

What is Boxing Day?

Boxing Day is a holiday celebrated on December 26 in the UK, Canada, Australia, and other Commonwealth countries. It originated as a day to give gifts to servants and tradespeople. Today, it's famous for massive retail sales and sporting events.

Why is it called Boxing Day?

The name likely comes from the tradition of giving "Christmas boxes" (gifts of money or goods) to servants and tradespeople. Another theory suggests it originates from alms boxes in churches distributed to the poor on this day.

Which countries celebrate Boxing Day?

Boxing Day is a public holiday in the UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Africa, Hong Kong, Nigeria, and other Commonwealth nations. While not an official holiday in the US, many retailers offer "Boxing Day" sales.
